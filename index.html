What is Aurora Client?

We are a free Minecraft 1.8.9 PVP client with a unique modern design that is easy to use.

Aurora is a modern PvP client with various improvements including performance optimizations, clean interfaces, and universal compatibility.

We have unique features you won't find elsewhere and customizability that is unrivaled.

Aurora Client was created to provide a modern, clean alternative to existing Minecraft clients with focus on performance and user experience.
